Specialized Care

Conditions We Treat

Comprehensive neurological therapy for various conditions affecting children and families

Cerebral Palsy

Improving motor function, coordination, and independence through targeted interventions.

Common Symptoms
  • Muscle stiffness or weakness
  • Difficulty with coordination
  • Balance problems
  • Involuntary movements
Our Treatments
  • Physical therapy for mobility
  • Occupational therapy for daily skills
  • Gait training and balance work

Autism Spectrum Disorder

Supporting communication, social skills, and sensory processing.

Common Symptoms
  • Difficulty with social communication
  • Repetitive behaviors
  • Sensory sensitivities
  • Challenges with routine changes
Our Treatments
  • Sensory integration therapy
  • Communication skill development
  • Social skills training

ADHD

Enhancing focus, attention, and executive function skills.

Common Symptoms
  • Difficulty focusing or paying attention
  • Hyperactivity and impulsiveness
  • Problems with organization
  • Difficulty following instructions
Our Treatments
  • Cognitive behavioral therapy
  • Executive function training
  • Attention and focus exercises

Developmental Delays

Supporting milestone achievement and developmental progress.

Common Symptoms
  • Delayed motor skills
  • Speech and language delays
  • Cognitive development delays
  • Social and emotional delays
Our Treatments
  • Early intervention therapy
  • Developmental milestone support
  • Family-centered care plans

Learning Disabilities

Addressing cognitive challenges and building academic skills.

Common Symptoms
  • Difficulty with reading or writing
  • Problems with math concepts
  • Memory and processing issues
  • Trouble following directions
Our Treatments
  • Cognitive skill development
  • Academic support strategies
  • Memory and processing training

Motor Disorders

Improving coordination, balance, and fine motor skills.

Common Symptoms
  • Poor coordination
  • Balance difficulties
  • Fine motor skill challenges
  • Muscle weakness or stiffness
Our Treatments
  • Motor skill development
  • Balance and coordination training
  • Strength and flexibility exercises
